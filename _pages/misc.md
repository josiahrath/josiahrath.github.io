A (partial) collection of [Herbert Gintis'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Herbert_Gintis) Amazon reviews. Reviews were retrieved from [Amazon Reviews 2023](https://amazon-reviews-2023.github.io/) and [Amazon Reviews Public Data Set](https://s3.amazonaws.com/amazon-reviews-pds/readme.html), the latter of which is no longer available from Amazon.

His work was and is immensely influential to my thinking. For more information, see this excellent [interview](https://www.umass.edu/preferen/You%20Must%20Read%20This/Gintis%20Colander%20Interview%202004.pdf) by David Colander or the [tributes](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/full/10.1177/04866134231218026) in *Review of Radical Political Economics*.
