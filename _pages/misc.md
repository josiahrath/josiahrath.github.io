### Herbert Gintis Reviews

A (partial) collection of [Herbert Gintis'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Herbert_Gintis) Amazon reviews. Reviews were retrieved from the [McAuley Lab dataset](https://amazon-reviews-2023.github.io/) and the [AWS S3 dataset](https://s3.amazonaws.com/amazon-reviews-pds/readme.html) (the latter is no longer available).

His work was and is immensely influential to my thinking. For more information, see this excellent [interview](https://www.umass.edu/preferen/You%20Must%20Read%20This/Gintis%20Colander%20Interview%202004.pdf) by David Colander or the [tributes](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/full/10.1177/04866134231218026) in *Review of Radical Political Economics*.

Note: Some reviews are truncated or missing authors/ratings/titles. Errors and floating text tags may remain due to merging issues, some of which was done manually. Thus, any remaining errors should be presumed to be mine (or Amazon’s) and not present when the review was written
